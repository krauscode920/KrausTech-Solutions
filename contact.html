Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical project timeline?

Most ML projects range from 2-8 weeks depending on complexity. We provide clear timelines during the discovery call.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

We work with both. What matters is that you have a real ML problem to solve and budget to invest in the solution. Our $1000 minimum makes us accessible to early-stage companies.

What if I'm not sure ML is the right solution?

That's exactly what the free discovery call is for. We'll give you an honest assessment—sometimes the answer is "you don't need ML for this."

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. We can discuss retainer arrangements for model monitoring, retraining, and updates after the initial deployment.

What industries do you work with?

We're industry-agnostic. If you have data and a business problem, we can likely help. Past work spans retail, SaaS, healthcare analytics, and fintech.
