Revenue Optimization

Dynamic Pricing Engine

ML-powered pricing system demonstrating $600K+ annual revenue impact

Purpose

Mountain Peak Bike Rentals operated with fixed pricing, leaving significant revenue on the table during high-demand periods while struggling to maintain bookings during slower times. The challenge was to implement an intelligent pricing system that could:

  • Maximize revenue during peak demand without losing customers
  • Maintain competitive pricing during off-peak periods
  • Respond to real-time market conditions (weather, day of week, seasonality)
  • Balance fleet utilization across different bike types

Business Goal: Transform from reactive, fixed pricing to proactive, data-driven revenue optimization that captures market willingness to pay while maintaining customer satisfaction.

How

1. Data Collection & Feature Engineering

Built a comprehensive dataset incorporating:

  • Historical booking patterns (2+ years of data)
  • Weather conditions (temperature, precipitation, cloud cover)
  • Seasonal indicators (peak, shoulder, off-season)
  • Day of week and time-based features
  • Real-time fleet capacity utilization

2. Machine Learning Architecture

Implemented separate Random Forest models for each bike type:

  • Mountain Bike Model: Optimized for $20-$80 price range
  • E-Bike Model: Calibrated for $30-$120 range
  • 100 decision trees per model for robust predictions
  • Feature importance analysis identified top pricing drivers

3. Business Intelligence Dashboard

Created an interactive Streamlit dashboard providing:

  • Real-time price recommendations based on current conditions
  • Revenue impact projections vs fixed pricing
  • Seasonal and weather-based analytics
  • Scenario planning tools for strategy testing
Python Scikit-learn Random Forest Pandas Streamlit Plotly

Results

$607K Additional Annual Revenue
+50.3% Revenue Increase
$830+ Avg Daily Increase
+63% Partly Cloudy Day Revenue

Key Performance Insights

  • Weather Optimization: Sunny days saw 48% revenue increases; system automatically reduced prices during poor weather to maintain bookings
  • Seasonal Intelligence: Peak season pricing maximized revenue while off-season remained competitive
  • Capacity Management: 90%+ utilization triggered premium pricing, low demand applied strategic discounts
  • Model Accuracy: MAE of $1.32-$1.70 for price predictions across both bike types

Business Impact

The dynamic pricing system transformed revenue economics without requiring additional fleet investment or operational changes. The $607K annual increase represents a 50.3% improvement over fixed pricing, with the system paying for itself within the first week of implementation.

Perhaps most importantly, the system provided data-driven confidence in pricing decisions, eliminating guesswork and enabling proactive revenue management.
